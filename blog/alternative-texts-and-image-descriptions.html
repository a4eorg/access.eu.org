What is Alt Text and Image Description (ID)?

Alt text is a short description that narrates the content of an image which is read out by screen readers, thus making them accessible to the visually impaired. This is also useful for people with certain sensory processing disorders and/or learning disabilities.

Image descriptions (IDs) can also be used. They are generally written in the caption or the comment section.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's

  • Be specific and concise
  • Be objective in your descriptions

Don'ts

  • Don't use emojis
  • Don't use opinion based adjectives
  • Don't begin with "picture of..." or "image of...", just describe the scene
  • Don't misuse the alt text feature by using it as a continuation of the caption
